Center for Advancement of Human Potential & Belonging (CAHPB)

A collaborative focused on research, consultation, and policy to expand access to healthcare and human needs across Will & Grundy counties and Illinois.

Call WGMC Read more at WGMC

Overview

CAHPB convenes universities, colleges, and healthcare partners with WGMC as the hub. The team collaborates on research and community consultation, analyzes data, and advances policies that strengthen the safety net for historically underserved groups.

Areas include racial health equity, LGBTQ+ health, behavioral health, access for the uninsured, and housing stability.
