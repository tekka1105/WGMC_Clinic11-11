Housing, Healthcare, and Hope (HHH)

A demonstration program that bridges healthcare and housing for people experiencing homelessness or housing insecurity.

Call for Help Read more at WGMC

Overview

HHH provides case management, care coordination, tenant-based rental assistance, medical respite, shelter referrals, and outreach. By addressing social determinants of health, the program helps participants stabilize housing and improve their health.

Get Started

  • Check eligibility on the home page
  • Bring any ID and medications if available
  • Call to speak with our team
How to Get Care

What Sets Us Apart

  • 24/7 care and support
  • Coordination of free transportation
  • Three daily meals with personalized plans
  • Independent living suites with kitchenettes
  • Special accommodations for single moms and couples
  • Onsite pharmacy and general healthcare services as needed
  • Average transitional placements of 90 days
Our coordinated care model streamlines services, improves outcomes, and eases transitions between providers.
