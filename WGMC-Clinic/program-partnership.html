Overview

WGMC restructured its care model to better meet community needs through a partnership with the Will County Community Health Center. Participants receive a triad of services: wellness and primary care, ancillary services, and access to medications.

This integration improves continuity, broadens access, and supports better outcomes for patients facing barriers to care.
